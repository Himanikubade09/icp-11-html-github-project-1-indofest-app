Navratri

About Navratri:

Navratri is a major Hindu festival celebrated for nine nights in honor of Goddess Durga and her nine divine forms. It symbolizes the victory of good over evil and is observed with great devotion across India. It marks the triumph of Goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura. Each day is dedicated to a different form of Devi (Shakti), from Shailputri to Siddhidatri. Devotees observe fasting, perform pujas, chant mantras, and engage in spiritual practices.
