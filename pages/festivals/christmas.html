Merry Christmas




Merry Christmas – The Festival of Joy and Peace 🎄✨

🎄 Decorations – People decorate their homes with Christmas trees, lights, wreaths, and ornaments. A star is often placed on top of the tree, symbolizing the Star of Bethlehem  
🕯️ Church Services – Christians attend midnight mass and special church prayers to celebrate the birth of Jesus Christ.  
🎁 Gift-Giving & Santa Claus – Exchanging gifts is a major tradition. Santa Claus (Saint Nicholas) is a popular figure who is said to bring gifts to children.  
🍪 Festive Feasts – Families enjoy special meals, including cakes, cookies, turkey, and other traditional delicacies.  
🎶 Carols & Songs – People sing Christmas carols like Jingle Bells, Silent Night, and We Wish You a Merry Christmas.
