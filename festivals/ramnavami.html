RAM NAVAMI :

Ram Navami is a Hindu festival that celebrates the birth of Lord Rama, the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It falls on the ninth day (Navami) of Chaitra month in the Hindu lunar calendar, which usually occurs in March or April. This festival marks the culmination of Chaitra Navaratri and holds immense significance, especially for followers of Sanatan Dharma (Hinduism). Ram Navami is not just a festival but a day to reflect on the virtues of Lord Rama‚Äîrighteousness, devotion, duty, and truth. His life teaches us the path of Dharma and Karma, encouraging us to lead a life of integrity and humility.

Best place to visit:

1.AYODHYA,(Uttar Pradesh)

Birthplace of Lord Rama, making it the most sacred place for Ram Navami. Grand celebrations at the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, with special aarti, bhajans, and kirtans. Devotees take a holy dip in the Sarayu River, believing it cleanses sins. Shobha Yatra (Grand Procession) featuring idols of Rama, Sita, Lakshmana, and Hanuman.
